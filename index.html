    How to Play Hangman by Lyssa

    1. Please kindly enter your name (Doesn't need to be real name) Then press enter or click start game to start playing.
    2. Guess the secret word one letter at a time based on the provided clue.
    3. Click the on-screen keyboard or use your real keyboard to play.
    4. Each wrong guess draws part of the hangman.
    5. You'll lose if the hangman is complete.
    6. You'll win if you guess all letters before the hangman is complete.
    7. Press P to play again after a game, or H to view history.
    8. Thank you for playing my game.
